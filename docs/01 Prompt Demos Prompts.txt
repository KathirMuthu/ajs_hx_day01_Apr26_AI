1. Zero-Shot Prompting (No Examples)
===
Prompt:
---
Summarize the following insurance claim in 5 bullet points, highlighting key facts, risks, and final decision:

Claim Details:
•	Policyholder: Rajesh Kumar 
•	Claim Type: Motor Insurance 
•	Incident: Accident reported on 12 Jan 
•	Damage: Front bumper and headlights 
•	Estimated Cost: ₹85,000 
•	Documents: FIR missing, photos submitted 
•	Initial Assessment: Suspicious timing (policy purchased 5 days before incident)

4. ReAct (Reason + Act / Multi-step Workflow Thinking)
===
Prompt:
---
You are an AI claims investigation agent.

Follow this process:
Step 1: Understand the claim details
Step 2: Identify missing or suspicious information
Step 3: Suggest what additional data should be retrieved (e.g., FIR, policy terms, claim history)
Step 4: Based on available + missing data, recommend next action (approve / reject / escalate for investigation)

Clearly separate your response into:
•	Reasoning 
•	Actions Required 
•	Final Recommendation
