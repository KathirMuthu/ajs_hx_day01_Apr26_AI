Demo 1: Sentence Completion (Classic)
===
Prompt:
Complete this sentence with the most likely word:
The insurance claim was rejected because of ___

Demo 2: Multiple Valid Answers (Probability Spread)
===
Prompt:
List 5 possible ways to complete this sentence:
The audit failed due to ___

Demo 3: Temperature / Creativity Effect (Run Twice)
===
Prompt:
Write one sentence explaining why a claim might be rejected.

Demo 4: Predict the Next Word Step-by-Step
===
Prompt:
Complete this step-by-step by predicting one word at a time:
‘The policyholder submitted a claim for…’

Then keep prompting:
What is the next most likely word?

Demo 5: Change Context → Change Prediction
===
Prompt 1:
Complete: The patient was given ___

Prompt 2:
In a criminal case, complete: The suspect was given ___

Demo 6: Bias in Probability (Business-Relevant)
===
Prompt:
Complete: The CEO decided to ___

Demo 7: Ranking Probabilities (Advanced but Simple)
===
Prompt:
Give 3 possible next words for:
‘The claim was flagged for ___’
and rank them by likelihood.
